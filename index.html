Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

How far in advance should I book Beat Baron? +
We recommend booking at least 4–8 weeks in advance for standard events, and 3–6 months ahead for weddings or large-scale events. Peak seasons fill up fast, so don't wait too long!
Can I request specific songs or genres? +
Absolutely! We encourage all clients to submit a must-play list and do-not-play list. We'll also have a pre-event consultation to understand your taste, audience demographics, and the vibe you're going for.
Do you provide your own equipment? +
Yes! All packages include professional DJ equipment. Higher-tier packages include premium sound systems, lighting, and effects. We handle setup and teardown — you don't need to worry about a thing.
What types of events do you cover? +
We cover weddings, corporate events, private parties, nightclubs, festivals, product launches, birthday parties, charity galas, and more. If it needs music, Beat Baron is there.
What is your cancellation policy? +
We require a non-refundable deposit to secure your date. Cancellations made 30+ days in advance will receive a partial refund of the remaining balance. Cancellations under 30 days are non-refundable. Full details are in our booking agreement.
Do you travel for out-of-city events? +
Yes, Beat Baron is available for events worldwide. Travel and accommodation costs are additional and will be discussed transparently during your booking consultation. We've performed across multiple countries and love destination events.
